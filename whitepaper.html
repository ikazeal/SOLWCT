1. 项目概述

WCT 是围绕赛事竞猜与社区互动构建的代币化产品。我们通过“积分竞猜 + 排行榜 + 奖励机制”的组合，为社区提供可视化、可参与、可复盘的赛季玩法。

2. 术语与定义

  • WCT：WorldCup Token，部署在 BNB Smart Chain 的代币。
  • 积分：用于竞猜与结算计算的计量单位，分为基础积分与持仓额外积分。积分不等同于法币或任何法定货币，不应被理解为可兑付债务或收益凭证。
  • 排行榜收益积分：仅统计竞猜胜利产生的“额外收益”（不含本金返还与持仓额外积分）。
  • 奖池（BNB）：用于活动奖励的 BNB 资金池。页面展示口径为“奖池地址的链上 BNB 余额”。

3. 产品形态与数据来源

3.1 当前实现形态

  • 前端站点：用于展示赛程、竞猜交互、排行榜与奖励信息。
  • 存储模式：可运行于“本地演示存储（浏览器 localStorage）”或“后端存储（API + 服务器持久化）”。具体以部署配置为准。
  • 链上读取：页面会通过 BSC RPC 读取合约与奖池地址数据（例如奖池地址 BNB 余额）。

3.2 赛程与比赛结果来源

  • 赛程优先读取站点内的 schedule.json。
  • 比赛结果可从第三方公开页面抓取并解析（存在结构变动导致失效的风险）。如数据源变更，以站点公告或更新后的实现为准。

4. 积分系统

4.1 基础积分

  • 初始化：钱包首次连接后获得 5000 基础积分。
  • 用途：用于下注本金、结算返还与收益分配计算。

4.2 WCT 持仓额外积分（不计入排行榜）

系统可根据钱包内 WCT 持仓自动计算“额外积分”。该部分：

  • 可用于下注本金（优先消耗）。
  • 不计入排行榜，避免因持仓导致排名失真。
  • 随持仓变化自动调整（定期刷新钱包余额）。

计算规则（阶梯）：

  • 持有 ≥ 10,000,000 WCT：额外积分 = 150
  • 持有 ≥ 5,000,000 WCT：额外积分 = 70
  • 其他：每 100,000 WCT 额外积分 = 10（向下取整）

5. 竞猜规则

  • 下注范围：单场最低 100 分，最高 500 分。
  • 封盘规则：比赛开始前 5 分钟 停止下注。
  • 下注限制：同一钱包同一场比赛仅可下注一次。
  • 可用积分：基础积分 +（持仓额外积分 - 已使用额外积分）。下注时先消耗额外积分，再消耗基础积分。

6. 结算与分配

每场比赛形成 3 个选项奖池：主胜 / 平局 / 客胜。结算规则：

  • 平局：所有下注者退回本金（不产生收益积分）。
  • 非平局：胜者按其下注占胜方总注的比例，瓜分败方奖池。
  • 收益积分：胜者获得的“额外收益”= 结算总返还 - 本金。

7. 排行榜口径（仅计算押注收益）

  • 计分来源：仅统计每场胜利结算产生的“额外收益积分”。
  • 不计入：基础积分本金返还、WCT 持仓额外积分、平局退回本金。

8. 奖池展示与获奖机制

8.1 奖池展示口径（链上同步）

  • 奖池地址：0x59cdafe58de6cdabf46900aff2d431d98e1c8503
  • 展示口径：页面显示该地址在 BNB Smart Chain 上的 BNB 余额（通过公开 RPC 读取）。
  • 重要说明：奖池余额不代表必然发放额度或必然收益，具体发放以每期活动规则与公告为准。

8.2 获奖机制（Top 5）

每期活动形成“总奖池”，用于排行榜奖励与生态增长。排行榜按“押注收益积分”排名，最终排名前 5 名可获得奖池奖励。

  • 第 1 名：总奖池的 40%
  • 第 2 名：总奖池的 20%
  • 第 3 名：总奖池的 10%
  • 第 4 名：总奖池的 5%
  • 第 5 名：总奖池的 5%

剩余 20%：用于活动营销与“每期比赛押中额外奖励”。具体发放时间、资格与规则以每期活动公告为准。

1. Overview

WCT is a tokenized product built around match predictions and community participation. The system combines points-based predictions, leaderboards, and campaign rewards to create a season-style community experience.

2. Definitions

  • WCT: WorldCup Token deployed on BNB Smart Chain.
  • Points: a unit of account used for predictions and settlement calculations. Points are not fiat money and should not be interpreted as a redeemable claim, debt, or guaranteed benefit.
  • Profit points (leaderboard): only the incremental profit generated from winning settlements (excluding principal refunds and holding bonus points).
  • Prize pool (BNB): BNB funds intended for campaign rewards. The website displays the on-chain BNB balance of a designated prize pool address.

3. Product Architecture & Data Sources

3.1 Implementation Modes

  • Front-end: displays fixtures, prediction UI, leaderboard, and rewards.
  • Storage: may run in a local demo mode (browser localStorage) or a server-backed mode (API + persistent storage), depending on deployment configuration.
  • On-chain reads: the website reads on-chain data via public BSC RPC endpoints (e.g., prize pool BNB balance).

3.2 Fixtures & Results

  • Fixtures are loaded from schedule.json when available.
  • Match results may be parsed from third-party public sources, which can change without notice and may cause settlement discrepancies.

4. Points System

4.1 Base Points

  • Initialization: a wallet receives 5000 base points on first connect.
  • Usage: used as prediction stake and for settlement distribution calculations.

4.2 WCT Holding Bonus Points (Excluded from Leaderboard)

To incentivize holding WCT, the system may compute bonus points automatically from the wallet’s WCT balance. Bonus points:

  • Can be used as prediction stake (consumed first).
  • Are excluded from the leaderboard to avoid distorted rankings.
  • Adjust automatically as holdings change (periodic balance refresh).

Tiered rule:

  • Hold ≥ 10,000,000 WCT: bonus points = 150
  • Hold ≥ 5,000,000 WCT: bonus points = 70
  • Otherwise: +10 points per 100,000 WCT (floored)

5. Prediction Rules

  • Stake range: 100–500 points per match.
  • Betting close: betting closes 5 minutes before kickoff.
  • One bet per match: one wallet can submit only one bet per match.
  • Available points: base points + (computed bonus - bonus spent). Bonus is consumed first, then base points.

6. Settlement & Distribution

Each match has three pools (Home / Draw / Away). Settlement rules:

  • Draw: everyone receives principal back (no profit points).
  • Non-draw: winners receive principal plus a proportional share of the losing pool.
  • Profit points: winners’ “extra profit” = total payout - principal.

7. Leaderboard Accounting (Profit Only)

  • Included: only profit points generated from winning settlements.
  • Excluded: principal returns, holding bonus points, and draw refunds.

8. Prize Pool Display & Winner Mechanism

8.1 On-chain prize pool display

  • Prize pool address: 0x59cdafe58de6cdabf46900aff2d431d98e1c8503
  • Display logic: the website displays the on-chain BNB balance of this address (read via public BSC RPC).
  • Important: the displayed balance is not a promise of payout. Actual distribution is determined by each campaign’s published rules.

8.2 Winner mechanism (Top 5)

Each campaign period forms a “total prize pool” used for leaderboard rewards and ecosystem growth. The leaderboard is ranked by profit points, and the top 5 wallets receive prize pool rewards.

  • Rank #1: 40% of the total prize pool
  • Rank #2: 20% of the total prize pool
  • Rank #3: 10% of the total prize pool
  • Rank #4: 5% of the total prize pool
  • Rank #5: 5% of the total prize pool

Remaining 20%: used for marketing campaigns and per-match extra rewards. Timing, eligibility, and exact rules will be announced per campaign.
